❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I receive my subscription credentials? +

After subscribing, your credentials are delivered instantly to your account inbox (My Subscriptions). You can view them securely from your dashboard at any time.

Are the shared accounts safe to use? +

Yes. Each credential is assigned exclusively to one subscriber per slot. We monitor accounts for misuse and provide replacements if credentials stop working.

What if my subscription stops working? +

Report the issue immediately using the "Report an Issue" button in your account menu. Our admin team will review and resolve or replace your subscription within 24 hours.

Can I get a refund? +

Refunds are available within 24 hours if the subscription credentials are non-functional due to a platform issue. Contact our support team with your order ID.
